# Training Methodology

## Objective

The objective of LIMBA is to investigate parameter-efficient fine-tuning techniques for a low-resource language while preserving the linguistic characteristics of Limba Sarda Comuna (LSC).

## Base Model

- Meta Llama 3.1
- Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (LoRA)
- Unsloth training framework

## Training Goals

- Improve Italian ↔ Limba Sarda Comuna translation
- Support conversational interaction
- Generate coherent Sardinian text
- Preserve grammatical consistency
- Explore NLP techniques for minority languages

## Current Status

The project is under continuous development and will be progressively expanded with additional datasets, evaluation procedures and linguistic improvements.

# Limitations and Responsible Use

## Model limitations

LIMBA is an experimental fine-tuned language model developed for Italian and Limba Sarda Comuna (LSC).

Users should consider the following limitations:

- the model may produce grammatically inconsistent or non-standard Sardinian;
- translation outputs may alter nuances, terminology or tone;
- performance may vary depending on the topic and prompt formulation;
- factual responses may contain inaccurate, incomplete or fabricated information;
- the model may mix Italian, LSC and other Sardinian linguistic forms;
- generated code may contain errors or require manual revision;
- repeated prompts may produce different responses;
- the current evaluation process is primarily qualitative and does not yet constitute a formal benchmark.

## Low-resource language considerations

Limba Sarda Comuna is a low-resource language in comparison with widely represented languages such as Italian and English.

Limited availability of high-quality training data may affect:

- vocabulary coverage;
- grammatical consistency;
- domain-specific terminology;
- translation fidelity;
- representation of regional linguistic variation.

LIMBA should therefore be considered an experimental contribution to minority-language NLP rather than an authoritative linguistic reference.

## Retrieval-assisted responses

The Hugging Face Spaces application can retrieve information from Sardinian and Italian Wikipedia.

Retrieval does not guarantee that:

- the selected page is the most relevant source;
- the retrieved information is complete or current;
- the generated response accurately represents the source;
- the translation preserves every factual detail.

Retrieved and generated information should be independently verified.

## Intended use

LIMBA is intended for:

- experimentation with minority-language NLP;
- Italian–LSC translation assistance;
- educational demonstrations;
- conversational interaction;
- Sardinian text generation;
- research and prototyping;
- testing fine-tuning and deployment workflows.

## Out-of-scope use

LIMBA should not be relied upon as the sole source for:

- medical, legal or financial decisions;
- emergency information;
- official translations;
- academic or professional fact-checking;
- safety-critical applications;
- authoritative linguistic certification.

## Human review

Human review is recommended for translations, educational materials, public communications and any content requiring linguistic or factual accuracy.
